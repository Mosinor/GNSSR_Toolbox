Data Clipping Tool

The second item in the applications’ drop-down menu is used for Data Clipping and Filtering. In this item user can select multiple polygonal regions over the oceans or land and retrieve all the available data over the selected regions during the period of interest. Our web-based toolbox facilitates the data retrieval for those who don’t have processing/downloading facilities to work with the CYGNSS’s big dataset.
